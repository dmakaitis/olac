The Omaha Lithuanian-American Community will be hosting a gala event, celebrating 70 years of preserving Lithuanian language, culture, customs, and traditions in Omaha. Please join us and our distinguished honorees.

The Belvedere
201 East 1st Street
Papillion, NE 68046

Cocktails - 5:30pm
Opening Remarks - 6:15pm
Dinner - 6:30pm
Special Greetings - 7:15pm
Program and Entertainment - 9:00pm

Cocktail attire is recommended.

Dinner will be catered by Aron Mackevicius and the Lithuanian Bakery who bring over 60 years of culinary experience to celebrate the Lithuanian American Community Anniversary. Owner and Executive Chef of Talus Spirits and Sustenance, Aron will share his flavors and techniques for us to savor and enjoy, including baked salmon, prime rib, potato and vegetable dishes. Little ones will enjoy delicious children’s options. Dessert will feature world-famous Napoleon torte.

An open cash bar will be hosted by the Polish American Community.

Entertainment will be provided by
DJ Arthur Bereisa, Nida Grigalavičiūtė, and the Aušra dance group.

Nida Grigalavičiūtė has been nominated for and received awards for her musical talent across a range of music from classical and blues to rock and jazz.

The Aušra dance group and their supporters have been an integral part of the Omaha Lithuanian community for over 50 years, sharing their love of dance and the desire to preserve their culture.

DJ Arthur Bereisa will be providing well known Lithuanian music to the audience.
