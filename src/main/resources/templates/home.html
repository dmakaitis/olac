The Lithuanian American Community of Omaha is proudly celebrating its 70th year as an organization since its founding on April 26th, 1953. A home to Lithuanians for decades, Omaha has more than just supported the Lithuanian community, the city has helped the organization thrive.

The Lithuanian American Community has been an integral part of Omaha history for over a century. We look forward to sharing this celebration with you, of not just culture, but the history that comes with it.
